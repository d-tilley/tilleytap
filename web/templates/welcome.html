Welcome...

TIlleYtAp is an integrated smart scale that measures the remaining content of a keg, handbuilt for use with my personal home kegerator. The data gathered from this scale is pushed to the cloud every 60 seconds, so you know not only what's on tap but also exactly how much is left. Stay connected to keep up to date with all of the local Indiana breweries that I have the pleasure of serving right from my home.
